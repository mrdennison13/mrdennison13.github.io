Approximating π using Monte Carlo

  • Take a square of length L and draw a circle of radius D = L inside.
  • Place points randomly inside the square: n_iterations.
  • Count the number that fall within the circle: n_over.
  • π can be calculated as: 4*n_over/n_iterations.
